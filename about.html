About

Driftocalypse consists of three brothers working to build drift cars for competition. Seth has a 1992 Nissan 240sx, Carson has a 2004 Nissan 350z and Gary has a 1992 Nissan 300zx. We all hope to be able to compete in the events near Indiana and Formula Drift. At the end of the day, we're just poor people with rich people dreams.
